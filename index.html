Buffalo Chicken Macaroni and Cheese

  1. 1 (12-ounce) can evaporated milk
  2. 1 cup milk
  3. ½ cup buffalo style hot sauce, such as Frank’s
  4. ¼ teaspoon garlic powder
  5. Salt & pepper to taste
  6. 3 cups shredded cheese (such as white cheddar, cheddar, or your favorite melting cheese)
  7. 1 cup finely chopped vegetables (such as celery, carrots, and/or onions)
  8. 1 pound precooked chicken, finely chopped
  9. ½ pound small uncooked pasta
